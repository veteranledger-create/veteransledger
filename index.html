PERSONAL ARCHIVE · GERMAN PERSPECTIVE

This is a private historical collection from my personal study of World War II. I am not an academic institution—just an individual researcher with deep respect for history and all those who lived through these events. My perspective focuses on German military history, presented with dignity and without demonization. Over two years of collecting documents, photographs, and personal accounts from archives across Europe.

This archive is a private collection of historical documents, photographs, and personal accounts from the period 1933–1945, compiled for educational purposes. It presents German military history from a documentary perspective, emphasizing factual accuracy and respectful remembrance. All materials are drawn from original sources and presented without political bias, strictly for historical study.
