# Transparency Policy — VeteranLedger

> **Date**: 2026-05-23  
> **Documentation of**: Sourcing methodology, AI imagery disclosure, editorial practices

---

## 1. Core Principles

VeteranLedger operates on the following transparency principles:

1. **Attribution completeness** — All sources credited, no copyright data removed
2. **AI disclosure** — AI-generated imagery clearly labeled
3. **Correction responsiveness** — All factual/attribution errors investigated promptly
4. **No hidden agenda** — Editorial independence, no political alignment
5. **Archival integrity** — Content preserved without distortion or sensationalism

---

## 2. Sourcing Methodology

### Primary Sources Used
| Source Type | Examples |
|-------------|---------|
| Official military records | OKH records, unit war diaries (Kriegstagebücher) |
| Contemporary photographs | Bundesarchiv collections, press agency archives |
| Government documents | Declassified intelligence reports, diplomatic cables |
| Personal correspondence | Letters, memoirs, contemporary diaries |

### Secondary Sources Used
| Source Type | Examples |
|-------------|---------|
| Academic monographs | University press publications, peer-reviewed journals |
| Reference works | Axis History Factbook, Lexikon der Wehrmacht |
| Documentary collections | US National Archives, Imperial War Museum |

### Archival Repositories
- Bundesarchiv (German Federal Archives) — Bild collections
- Wikimedia Commons — Public Domain and CC-licensed media
- National Archives (USA, UK) — declassified records

## 3. AI Imagery Disclosure Framework

### Decision Tree for Image Selection

```
Is authentic archival imagery available?
├── YES → Is it under an appropriate license?
│   ├── YES → Use authentic image with full attribution
│   └── NO  → Can rights-holder be contacted?
│       ├── YES → Request permission
│       └── NO  → Consider AI reconstruction with disclosure
└── NO  → Does the subject require visual representation?
    ├── YES → AI reconstruction labeled as such
    └── NO  → Text-only description
```

### Labeling Requirements

Every AI-generated image must include:
1. **Visual badge**: `<span class="attr-badge">AI Reconstruction</span>` in the attribution block
2. **Text disclosure**: In the caption, e.g., "Archival reconstruction — no authentic photograph known to exist"
3. **License note**: AI imagery is original creation, not licensed from third parties

### Replacement Policy

As the public-domain corpus expands through:
- Copyright expirations
- New archival digitization projects
- Rights-holder permissions

...AI-assisted images will be progressively replaced with authentic archival material. The `transparency-policy.html` page tracks this commitment.

## 4. Editorial Practices

### Content Selection
- Entries are selected for historical significance, not political bias
- Both well-known and lesser-known figures/events are included
- Controversial topics are addressed with factual neutrality
- Opposing perspectives are cited where relevant

### Language Standards
- Neutral, factual tone in documentary descriptions
- Historical terminology preserved for accuracy (e.g., period-appropriate unit names)
- No glorification or demonization — presentation as historical record
- Clear separation of fact from analysis

### Fact-Checking Process
1. Cross-reference with at least two independent sources
2. Verify dates, names, and locations against official records
3. Note uncertainty where sources conflict
4. Update entries as new scholarship becomes available

## 5. Correction & Removal Process

See `removal-requests.html` for the full process. In summary:

| Concern Type | Response Time | Action |
|-------------|---------------|--------|
| Factual error | 15 business days | Correction with source citation |
| Attribution error | 10 business days | Updated credit line |
| Copyright concern | 5 business days | Remove or replace pending review |
| Ethical concern | 15 business days | Review with contextual framing or removal |

---

## 6. Attribution Integrity Guarantee

VeteranLedger guarantees that:
- **No attribution information is removed** from any image
- **No license terms are altered** from their original form
- **No copyright data is simplified** beyond the original statement
- **All Creative Commons requirements** (BY, SA, etc.) are fully honored
- **Public Domain dedications** are clearly marked as such

The only changes made to attribution are:
- Presentation format (compact → expandable)
- Layout (multi-line → collapsible hierarchy)

## 7. Independence Statement

VeteranLedger:
- Has no political, governmental, or military affiliation
- Receives no funding from any external organization
- Has no editorial input from any outside entity
- Does not accept sponsored content or advertisements
- Is operated for educational and archival purposes only
