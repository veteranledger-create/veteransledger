Privacy Policy

VeteranLedger Historical Archive

1. Scope

This privacy policy applies to the VeteranLedger website (the "Archive"), an educational historical documentary project. We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining your trust. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. No Personal Data Collection

VeteranLedger does not collect, store, or process any personal data. We do not:

  • Require registration or account creation
  • Collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Track individual browsing behavior
  • Use analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Deploy tracking cookies or fingerprinting technologies
  • Collect IP addresses in persistent logs

3. Cookies

This website uses functional cookies only:

  • Theme preference — stores your light/dark mode selection in localStorage. No data is transmitted to servers.
  • Cookie notice dismissal — records that you have acknowledged this notice (localStorage, 1 year expiry). No data is transmitted to servers.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. All stored data remains on your device and is never sent to any server.

4. Third-Party Content

This archive displays images hosted on third-party platforms (including Wikimedia Commons and Bundesarchiv) for educational and archival purposes under applicable licenses (Public Domain, Creative Commons). When your browser requests these images, the hosting platform may receive your IP address and standard HTTP headers, subject to their own privacy policies. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the data practices of these third parties.

5. External Links

This website links to external sources for citation and attribution purposes. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any external websites they visit.

6. Data Security

This website is served over HTTPS, ensuring encrypted communication between your browser and our server. All pages are statically generated; no user data is stored on any server.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection regulations (including GDPR and CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold (we hold none)
  • Request deletion of any personal data (there is none to delete)
  • Object to data processing (no processing occurs)
  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please use the Removal Requests page.

9. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically.
